In memory of my dad, Raphael Kirui

Daddy

Daddy

I know I must be lucky to have you as my Dad, since my childhood to now....

I won't rest untill I accomplish what we plan and started together.

It is not easy to comprehend a new life without your lead, I am grateful for the time you have stood with me.

Now you are gone, but you will forever live in my heart.

You showed a true leadership spirit, a compare the way you led us with how Moses led the Israelites.

You were such a selfless Dad, you did everything to our family.

Forever live in my heart

We won't see you now but I know you are with us.
