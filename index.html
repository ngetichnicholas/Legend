Daddy

To My Dad

I must be lucky to have you as my Dad, since my childhood to now....

You was my protector, a guiding hand, a best friend, and a superhero. The incredible memories you shared with us and the amazing father you were, still bring light into our darker days.

Despite the loss, I celebrate you and grateful for the years you have been with us.

You were the guiding light in my life, and I am reminded how lucky I was to have you.

It is not easy to start a new life without your lead, I am grateful for the time you stood with us.

You will forever be in my heart.Because in there you are still alive

You showed a true leadership spirit, that was legendary.

You were such a selfless Dad, you did everything for us.

Rest in peace Dad,

A great soul never dies. It brings us together again and again.
